## Privacy

The dataset contains only aggregate counts and histograms derived from
user-contributed trip summaries that passed a k-anonymity threshold of k=5.
No individual trip, route, or user identifier is included. See
`docs/superpowers/specs/2026-04-13-impact-dashboard-design.md` for the full
anonymization pipeline.
