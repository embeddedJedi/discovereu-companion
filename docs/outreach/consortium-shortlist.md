# Consortium Candidate Longlist — DiscoverEU Companion KA2

> Draft shortlist of 15 candidate youth organisations across DE/ES/PL/IT/FR/PT/GR for a KA2 Cooperation Partnership.
> Status key: **verified** = confirmed active youth-field org with public Erasmus+ footprint; **verify** = plausible fit, requires desk-check before outreach.
> Sources: SALTO-YOUTH Otlas partner-finding database, Erasmus+ Project Results Platform, national agency partner directories (JUGEND für Europa DE, INJUVE ES, FRSE PL, Agenzia Nazionale Giovani IT, Agence Erasmus+ Jeunesse & Sport FR, Agência Nacional Erasmus+ Juventude em Acção PT, IKY GR), ESN national chapter listings.
