DiscoverEU Companion

An Open-Source Trip Planning Tool for European Youth

Engage · Connect · Empower

What is it?

DiscoverEU Companion is a free, open-source web application that helps young Europeans plan, budget, and navigate their DiscoverEU rail trip across 33 participating countries. Designed with accessible and inclusive principles, it lowers the threshold for participation — especially for youth with fewer opportunities — while promoting sustainable mobility and intercultural understanding across Europe.

Why it matters

  • Inclusion: Built-in accessibility layer (Wheelmap integration), LGBTQ+ safety map (ILGA data), and a “fewer opportunities” budget mode — ensuring no young traveler is left behind.
  • Sustainability: CO₂ vs. flying calculator shows the environmental benefit of rail travel, reinforcing the programme’s green mobility message.
  • Digital empowerment: Fully open-source (MIT license), no installation required, works offline as a PWA — accessible to anyone with a browser.

5 Unique Features

  1. Mandatory reservation warnings — alerts travelers to routes requiring advance booking, preventing missed trains and penalty fares
  2. 4 seat-credit tracker — visualizes the DiscoverEU credit system so travelers optimize their free journeys
  3. CO₂ savings calculator — compares rail vs. flight emissions with a shareable “Green Traveler” badge
  4. Accessibility & LGBTQ+ safety layers — Wheelmap data + ILGA Rainbow Map scores integrated into the interactive map
  5. AI route planner — natural language trip suggestions powered by open LLM APIs (user-provided keys, no data collection)
