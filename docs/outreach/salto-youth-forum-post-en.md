# SALTO Youth — Partner-Finding Post

**Title:** Seeking partners — KA2 Cooperation Partnership on inclusive DiscoverEU trip planning (open-source, live pilot)

**Body:**

We are looking for 2–3 partner organisations from Programme Countries to co-apply for an **Erasmus+ KA2 Cooperation Partnership (Youth field)** built around an already-live, open-source DiscoverEU trip-planning companion.

The pilot is shipped, MIT-licensed, and inspectable today:
- Demo: https://embeddedjedi.github.io/discovereu-companion/
- Code: https://github.com/embeddedJedi/discovereu-companion

The tool lowers the threshold for participation in DiscoverEU for youth with fewer opportunities: Schengen-visa checklist, reservation-penalty warnings, accessibility overlay (Wheelmap), Rainbow Map, CO2 footprint badge, offline PWA, multi-language UI. A Turkish inclusion layer is already built in as a strength — supporting non-EU Programme-Country youth end-to-end.

**Planned project:** 24 months, ~€300k, priorities: Inclusion + Digital + Green + Participation.

**Looking for partners who can:** contribute local youth testing, run focus groups, disseminate through national networks, and co-author deliverables.

**You will receive:** budget share, co-authorship, shared MIT IP, featured partner placement.

Write to: partners@discovereu-companion.eu (or reply here).
